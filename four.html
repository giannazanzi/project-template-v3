In hopes of encouraging more voting-aged Americans to register for the upcoming Presidential election, this campaign, ‘This Goat Says VOTE!’ uses a zany slogan and brightly-colored, Risograph-printed posters and buttons to catch the attention of anyone who might walk by. This campaign’s key demographic is for young adults that are not yet registered to vote.
